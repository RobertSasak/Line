Bio

Line Stokland Røthing is a 23 year old singer-songwriter from Bergen, Norway. Her music lies between pop, jazz and the singer-songwritertradition. She started singing and making music when she was 10 years old, but the guitar didn't arrive until she was 15. Her voice is the center of the music, guiding you through stories of love, heartbreak and fantasy.
